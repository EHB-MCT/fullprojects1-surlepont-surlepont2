LES AMIS DU PONT #5

5 / 17

Jef, Katrien, Arnout and Djaina also meet regularly on our bridge. Their story parallels that of countless others we met in recent weeks: they discovered this place especially during the lockdowns, in which the lack of quality green space in our city became so evident. There has been a lot of talk in the past year about the need for perspective, and let's face it, perspective is plentiful here. In addition, a summer with fewer worries looms at the end, and even an outdoor swimming pool.
